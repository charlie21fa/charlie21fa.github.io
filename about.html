About Me!

My name is Carlos Flores-Angel, or as most people call me, "Charlie".
I was born in San Diego, California and moved around a lot while growing up, living in southern California, northern Mexico,
central Mexico, and Wisconsin.




I am an undergrad student at URI double majoring in History and Anthropology. Some of my main passions are literature,
music, and cooking. One of my major accomplishments is having started a baking business at 15 with my mom that is still
run today by my older sister. I enjoy discussions with my peers on these topics and academic subjects. Some of my main
academic interests are Latin American studies, ancient history, and non-Eurocentric historical accounts.
